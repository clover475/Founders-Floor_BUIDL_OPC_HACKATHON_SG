# Product

## Summary

Founders' Floor is a virtual office for one-person companies. It recreates the small coworker moments solo founders lose: seeing who is around, asking “can you take a quick look?”, taking a short break together, and having shipped work noticed.

Core message:

> AI lets one person run a company. A one-person company should not mean working alone.

## Users

| Stage | Situation | Useful outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Exploring | No clear business idea yet | Find a topic and a person to discuss it with |
| Ideating | Has an early idea | Clarify user and problem |
| Building | Creating a product or service | Get quick product or technical feedback |
| Launching | Looking for users | Test messaging and outreach |
| Operating | Has users or revenue | Discuss growth and operating problems |

No user is required to provide a company, product, or contact details.

## Core experience

```text
Clock In
→ Choose a room and one outcome
→ See coworkers and their availability
→ Complete one lightweight interaction
→ Take an optional shared break or play Elevator Pitch
→ Clock Out with evidence of progress
→ Appear on the Ship Wall
```

## MVP scope

- Lobby with office rooms and live-looking activity statistics.
- Clock In without login.
- Idea, Build, Feedback, and Growth rooms.
- Visible member presence and availability state.
- One structured Desk Check flow with templates and status changes.
- Break Room with Move Together and Elevator Pitch.
- Clock Out form and Ship Wall.
- Seeded demo members plus local current-user data.

## Demo-critical scope

If time is limited, preserve these in order:

1. Clock In.
2. Office room with coworkers.
3. Desk Check state change.
4. Clock Out.
5. Ship Wall update.
6. Elevator Pitch timer and feedback card.
7. Move Together.

## Out of scope

- Login, profiles, payment, annual membership.
- Real-time audio/video or chat.
- Automated matching or AI recommendations.
- Supabase and cross-device synchronization.
- Full forum, knowledge base, or weekly Sprint system.
- 3D map, avatars, points, or many games.

## Acceptance signals

- A new user can clock in within 60 seconds.
- A user can understand who is available and what each person is working on.
- A Desk Check can move from open to accepted to completed.
- A user can clock out and immediately see the result on the Ship Wall.
- At least one participant reports feeling “less like I was working alone.”
- Demo works without network access after the page has loaded.

## Long-term direction

The office is the daily entry point to a broader OPC practice community: topic forums, weekly sprints, product testing, contribution reputation, case library, local gatherings, and founder circles. These are roadmap items, not MVP dependencies.
